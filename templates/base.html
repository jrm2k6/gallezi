About Gallezi

Gallezi (contracted from Gallery and Prezi) provides an easy way to display Prezis. It also offers a vote system which could use in a design contest or something equivalent.
Gallezi has been developed and designed by Jeremy Dagorn.
It is open source and you can clone and modify it at your convenience.
